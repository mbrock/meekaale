📰 Wholehearted Choices and “morality as Taxes”

Author: Joe

Full Title: Wholehearted Choices and “morality as Taxes”

URL: https://handsandcities.com/2020/12/12/wholeheartedness-and-morality-as-taxes/

Highlights from March 2nd, 2021.

Morality, in this conception, is sort of like taxes; it’s invades your personal domain — the place where you make choices based on what you want and care about — and then takes some amount of stuff for its own ends. One hopes it isn’t too much; one wonders how little one can get away with paying, without breaking “the rules.”
