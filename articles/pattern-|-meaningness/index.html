Author: David Chapman

Full Title: Pattern | Meaningness

URL: https://meaningness.com/pattern

Highlights from February 25th, 2021.

Pattern is what makes the world interpretable—what makes it make sense. Perceiving pattern is needed for all effective action—whether you are a person or a bug.
Patterns are everywhere in our experience. The material world is full of patterns: shapes, processes, connections, similarities and differences. Society, culture, thought, and concepts are also patterned.
