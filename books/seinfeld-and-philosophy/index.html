📙 Seinfeld and Philosophy

Author: William Irwin

Full Title: Seinfeld and Philosophy

Highlights from March 2nd, 2021.

This “show about nothing” is indeed philosophical in its own way. The very name ‘Seinfeld’ rings with philosophical significance, calling to mind (for some of us, at least) Martin Heidegger’s Sein und Zeit (Being and Time), and meaning (roughly) “field of being” in German. Seinfeld has indeed been fertile ground, a “field of being” from which spring fruitful discussions and observations of the philosophical importance of the mundane.
