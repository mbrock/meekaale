Sacredness

  • The Long Path that Leads from the Making of Our World to God

    Further, I have now come to the view that the Sacredness of the physical world – and the potential of the physical world for sacredness – is a powerful, surprising, and sure path to recognizing, and providing small steps towards understanding the existence of God, whatever God may be, as a necessary part of the reality of the universe.
